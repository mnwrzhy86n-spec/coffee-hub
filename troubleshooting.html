Troubleshooting

One rule: change one thing at a time, and only for the next shot. This one is already made — drink it.

😖 Too sour under-extracted

Sharp, lemony, thin. The water didn't pull enough out of the coffee.

→

Grind finer

→

Or let the shot run a few seconds longer

→

Check the machine was fully warmed up

😣 Too bitter over-extracted

Harsh, ashy, dries your mouth. The water pulled out too much.

→

Grind coarser

→

Or stop the shot a few seconds earlier

→

Old, stale beans also taste bitter — check the bag date

💨 Too fast under 20 seconds

Gushes out pale and watery.

→

Grind finer

→

Check the dose — was it really 18 g?

→

Tamp level — a tilted puck lets water sneak past one side

🐌 Too slow over 35 seconds

Drips reluctantly, or barely comes out at all.

→

Grind coarser

→

Tamp lighter

→

Too much coffee in the basket — weigh the dose again

🫥 No crema

The shot comes out flat, no golden layer on top.

→

Fresh beans matter most — crema fades weeks after roasting

→

Grind finer if the shot also ran fast

🫧 Milk gone wrong

Big bubbles, or paper-thin milk with no texture.

→

Big bubbles: wand tip too shallow — swirl and tap the jug to rescue it

→

No foam at all: wand tip too deep — start with it just under the surface

→

Screams while steaming: cold jug pressed too hard against the wand — lower it slightly
