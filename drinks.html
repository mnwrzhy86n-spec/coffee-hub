Milk Drinks

Every drink below starts with a double espresso. The only difference is what goes on top.

Espresso

Just the shot · small cup

espresso

Ratio: espresso only, ~36 g out from 18 g in

Serve: straight away — crema fades fast

Americano

Espresso + hot water · mug

espresso
hot water

Ratio: 1 part espresso, 3–4 parts hot water

Tip: water in the mug first, then the shot on top keeps the crema

Common mistake: boiling water — just-off-the-boil is smoother

Latte

Milky and mild · tall glass or mug

espresso
steamed milk
foam

Ratio: 1 part espresso, 4 parts milk, thin foam layer

Milk: silky, barely any foam — pour from low, slowly

Common mistake: milk too hot; it should be drinkable immediately

Flat White

Stronger latte · small cup

espresso
steamed milk

Ratio: 1 part espresso, 2 parts milk, almost no foam

Milk: glossy "wet paint" texture, no visible bubbles

Common mistake: too much milk — it's meant to taste of coffee

Cortado

Half and half · small glass

espresso
steamed milk

Ratio: equal parts espresso and warm milk

Milk: warm and smooth, not foamy

Common mistake: steaming a huge jug for such a small drink

Cappuccino

The foamy one · medium cup

espresso
milk
foam

Ratio: thirds — espresso, steamed milk, thick foam

Milk: stretch it longer at the start for a deep foam cap

Common mistake: stiff, dry bubbles — good foam still pours
